Hypothalamus

The hypothalamus is located just above the brain stem and below the thalamus. Its four main functions are to:

  • Regulate body temperature

  • Regulate rate of respiration and heart beat

  • Make you aware of hunger and thirst

  • Regulate sex drive

Three hormones that the hypothalmus secretes are:

  • HGH (Human Growth Hormone)

  • Oxytocin

  • Prolactin
